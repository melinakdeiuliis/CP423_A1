As a foodie enthusiast, these people can often find themselves spending hours over the internet looking for the best recipe out there. Thats why a team of developers created a search engine system that could help people find exactly what they are looking for called RecipeFinder. This search engine is designed for finding the best recipes while taking into account specific ingredients, preferences and dietary restrictions. 
RecipeFinder allows foodie enthusiasts and at home chefs to search by ingredients, cuisine, meal names and more. The search engine uses clustering to group similar recipes together allowing the user to easily browse recipe options that best suite the client. By using advanced natural language processing the results that show up can be personalized through the best recommendation according to the search query using their search history and other preferences. RecipeFinder saves at home chefs lots of time by and allows foodie enthusiasts to explore new foods.
